Melisa Tran (she/her)

Sundaze (2022)“Sundaze” showcases scenes of my life as I continue growing as a person and an adult. From navigating heartbreak and loss to working and taking care of myself, I bring my inner child along with me, nurturing and making room for her. Cartoons will always have my heart; they are my go-to for self soothing. I hope the viewer sees or incorporates bits of joy in their everyday lives.
